# Shift / Assignment Rules — by Site

This document lists every assignment type the point tracker recognizes for each site, along with how compensation is calculated. Send this to site supervisors to confirm we're handling each shift correctly before unlocking the site for users.

**Universal rules** (apply at every site unless noted otherwise):

- **AR rate (general):** $20/hr base, with standard time-of-day multipliers — weekday after 17:00 ×1.10, weekday after 23:00 ×1.25, weekend ×1.10 until 17:00 then ×1.25, holidays ×1.25 throughout.
- **AR rate (OB):** $13/hr base, same multipliers as general.
- **Unrestricted call (pager) pay:** $3.5/hr during the unrestricted-call window (where applicable). Standard amounts work out to 49 pts weekday (14hr × 3.5) and 84 pts weekend (24hr × 3.5).
- **Placement minimum:** OR / ASC / NORA / placement-style shifts get a guaranteed 4hr / 80pt minimum when clocked in (at the general AR rate base).
- **Forced Off:** flat 56 pts (no time required).
- **Vacation / Off / No Call / Off Grid / Off Post Call / Job Share Off / Locums Coverage / Part Timer:** 0 pts. Calendar markers only.
- **Subspecialty coverage (cardiac/liver):** when the shift exposes the subspec checkbox and you toggle it on, +45 pts/day flat.
- **OB production doubling:** non-OR cases entered on an OB shift have their production points doubled.

## Notes for supervisors

If anything in your site's section above doesn't match how compensation actually works for your group, please flag the specific shift and what should change. Common things to confirm:

1. **Pager hours** for each call shift (start/end on weekdays vs weekends).
2. **Unrestricted call eligibility** — when (if ever) does the 3.5/hr unrestricted rate apply during this shift?
3. **AR rate base** — 20/hr (general), 13/hr (OB), or something custom.
4. **Production doubling** — any non-OB shifts that should double production?
5. **Subspecialty bonuses** — any cardiac/liver/etc. shift that should expose the +45 pt subspec checkbox?
6. **Placement minimums** — any placement shifts where the 4hr / 80pt minimum doesn't apply?
7. **Flat-pay shifts** — any shifts paid as a fixed amount per day with no AR / case calc (like IMC's AOD at 235 pts)?
8. **Forced off** — confirm 56 pts flat. Any local variants?
9. **Off-type calendar labels** — any zero-point markers we're missing or that should pay differently?
